Left Handed Piano

This is the webMidi version of the Kundalini Piano Mirror. It creates a left-handed (mirror image piano).

For more information, please see:

https://www.kundalinisoftware.com/kundalini-piano-mirror/


Make sure to turn off local echo on your digital piano. Then select your tranposition mode.

For some scores, checkout Bach-In-The-Mirror
